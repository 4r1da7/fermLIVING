Boutique

At our brand house in the heart of Copenhagen, you’ll find a universe of all things ferm LIVING. Housing our offices as well as our first-ever retail space, it’s a universe that welcomes you home, that embraces the contrasts of life and creates an authentic and informal atmosphere that is at once intimate and inspiring - a space where you can always feel comfortably you.

  1. 1/5 The Home of ferm LIVING

    Nestled between picturesque canals, quirky houseboats and the untamed freetown Christiania, Kuglegården 1-5 is a historic building that dates all the way back to 1770, and whose uncompromising façade creates a beautiful contrast to its unpolished backdrop.

    Our Boutique is a 450m² space that makes up the entire ground floor of our Brand House. The stately building was originally used as an arsenal for the Danish military, and has been under military tenure ever since. That is, up until 2019, when ferm LIVING began a complete and careful renovation of the building’s 1900m².

    Our vision has always been to create a space for our customers to feel authentically and comfortably themselves, and our new home is no exception.

    Open to private consumers as well as wholesale clients and the press, you can shop the entire ferm LIVING collection as well as a curated selection of vintage curio, plants, coffee table books and other accessories for the home. On the floors above are our offices, where our creative and operational staff do their best work. Here, our team of ferm LIVING designers are experimenting with new ideas and creating new collections of furniture, lighting and accessories for the home, only steps away from our end consumers.

  4. 4/5 Library

    At the Material Library at the Home of ferm LIVING, we have created a creative workspace where you can find material swatches of all of our Made to Order products, source colour inspiration as well as touch and experience all of the different textures and surfaces from our collection. You will also find 3D-printed, 1:10 models of our pieces of furniture so that you can do concrete spatial planning for your upcoming projects. You can even create your own personal moodboard using the trays available.

    You can book an hour in the Material Library by sending an email to themateriallibrary@fermliving.com with the following information:

    • - Name
    • - Desired time slot
    • - A short description of your project (please include whether it is private or professional)
